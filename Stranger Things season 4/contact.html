Stranger Things

Stranger Things is an American science fiction horror streaming television series created by the Duffer Brothers and streaming on Netflix. The series is set in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, during the 1980s, and centers on the investigation into the disappearance of a young boy (Will Byers) amid supernatural events occurring around the town, including the appearance of a girl with psychokinetic abilities (Eleven). The series has been praised for its characterization, atmosphere, acting, soundtrack, directing, writing, and homages to 1980s films. It has also been a commercial success for Netflix, becoming one of its most-watched shows.

Season 1

The first season of Stranger Things begins in November 1983 with the disappearance of Will Byers, a young boy from the town of Hawkins, Indiana. His mother, Joyce, and his friends, Mike, Lucas, and Dustin, begin to search for him, but they soon discover that more is going on than meets the eye. Will has been abducted by a creature from the Upside Down, an alternate dimension that is connected to Hawkins. The Upside Down is a dark and dangerous place, inhabited by monstrous creatures. Meanwhile, a young girl with psychokinetic abilities named Eleven escapes from Hawkins National Laboratory, a government facility where she was being experimented on. Eleven joins forces with Mike, Lucas, Dustin, and Joyce to find Will and save him from the Upside Down.

Season 2

The second season of Stranger Things begins one year after the events of the first season. Will has been rescued from the Upside Down, but he is still struggling with the effects of his experience. Meanwhile, a new threat emerges in Hawkins. A mysterious entity called the Mind Flayer is trying to break through into the real world and unleash its evil on Hawkins. Eleven, Mike, Lucas, Dustin, and Joyce must once again band together to protect Hawkins and its residents from the Mind Flayer and the Upside Down.

Season 3


The third season of Stranger Things begins in the summer of 1985. Hawkins is enjoying a period of peace, but the Mind Flayer is still lurking in the Upside Down, waiting for an opportunity to strike again. Meanwhile, the people of Hawkins are preparing for the opening of a new mall called Starcourt Mall. The mall is the biggest thing to ever happen to Hawkins, but it also brings with it a new danger. The Mind Flayer has begun to possess people through a new type of creature called a Flayed. The Flayed are mindless drones that are controlled by the Mind Flayer. Eleven, Mike, Lucas, Dustin, and Joyce must once again band together to stop the Mind Flayer and protect Hawkins from its evil plans.

Season 4


The fourth season of Stranger Things is divided into two volumes, with the first volume released on May 27, 2022, and the second volume released on July 1, 2022. The fourth season takes place six months after the events of the third season. The Battle of Starcourt has left Hawkins in shambles, and the town is still recovering from its wounds. The friends are now separated for the first time, and they are all struggling to deal with the aftermath of the battle. Eleven has lost her powers, and she is trying to find a way to get them back. Meanwhile, a new and even more terrifying threat emerges in Hawkins. A new villain named Vecna is using his powers to target the town's youth. Eleven, Mike, Lucas, Dustin, and Joyce must once again band together to defeat Vecna and protect Hawkins from his evil plans.
